NUTELLA CHEESECAKE
by Nigella. Featured in NIGELLISSIMA
INTRODUCTION
250 grams digestive biscuits
75 grams soft unsalted butter
1 x 400 grams jar nutella (at room temperature)
100 grams chopped toasted hazelnuts
500 grams cream cheese (at room temperature)
60 grams icing sugar (sifted)
METHOD
1. Break the digestives into the bowl of a processor, add the butter and a 15ml tablespoon
of Nutella, and blitz until it starts to clump. Add 25g/3 tablespoons of the hazelnuts and
continue to pulse until you have a damp, sandy mixture.
2. Tip into a 23cm/9inch round springform and press into the base either using your
hands or the back of a spoon. Place in the fridge to chill.
3. Beat the cream cheese and icing sugar until smooth and then add the remaining
Nutella to the cream cheese mixture, and continue beating until combined.
